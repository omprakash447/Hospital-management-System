Services

SUM Hospital is one of the largest Hospitals in Odisha, providing General and Super
Speciality medical services at affordable rates. It provides Super Speciality services
including Cardiothoracic and Vascular Surgery, Cardiology, Critical Care Medicine,
Endocrinology, Gastroenterology (Medical & Surgical), Immunology & Rheumatology,
Oncology (Medical & Surgical),Nephrology, Neurology, Neurosurgery, Pediatric Surgery,
Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery, Hematology, Urology and Neonatology.
